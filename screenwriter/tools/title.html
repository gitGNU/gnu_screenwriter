Screenplay-tool

screenplay-title

Most screenplay writing applications feature a Title Page generator which will create an industry-standard title page for you. Screenplay-title is a method of doing that, although of course title pages can be generated manually just as easily, using any text editor.

To generate a properly formatted title page for your screenplay, use:

$ screenplay-title

Which interactively creates and saves the file title.page with a capitalized and centered title, a correctly placed by-line, and your contact info.
